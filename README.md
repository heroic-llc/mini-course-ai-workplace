# AI in the Workplace

A writingIO mini course on professional AI use, policies, and workplace communication.

## Course Overview

Learn how to use AI professionally while navigating workplace policies, communicating effectively with colleagues, and building sustainable habits.

## Lessons

1. **AI at Work** - Introduction to professional AI use
2. **Workplace Policies** - Understanding and following guidelines
3. **Communicating About AI** - Talking with colleagues effectively
4. **Professional Use Cases** - Where AI helps at work
5. **Building AI into Your Workflow** - Sustainable integration

## Target Audience

- Professionals using or considering AI at work
- Employees navigating workplace AI policies
- Anyone wanting to use AI professionally and appropriately
- Teams developing AI practices together
