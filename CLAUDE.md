# CLAUDE.md - Project Instructions

## Course Overview
AI in the Workplace - A writingIO mini course on professional AI use, policies, and communication.

## Audience
- Professionals using AI at work
- Employees navigating AI policies
- Anyone using AI professionally
- Teams developing AI practices

## Tone and Style
- Professional and practical
- Balanced—acknowledges both benefits and considerations
- Respectful of organizational needs

## Video Script Guidelines
- Brand name is "writingIO" (not "Writing IO" or "writing.io")
- Spell out numbers and acronyms for speech (e.g., "A I" not "AI")
- Scripts must be under 5000 characters for HeyGen
- First lesson includes Amanda intro and AI disclosure

## Key Themes
- Professional AI use differs from personal use
- Policies and transparency matter
- Good communication builds trust
- Sustainable habits over occasional use
