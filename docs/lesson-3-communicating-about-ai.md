# AI in the Workplace

## Lesson 3: Communicating About AI

### Overview
AI affects how you work with others. Good communication builds trust.

---

### Communication Principles

**Be transparent when appropriate**
- Disclose AI use when relevant
- Build trust and set expectations

**Explain what AI does**
- Help colleagues understand
- Clear up misconceptions

**Don't oversell**
- Be honest about limitations
- Acknowledge potential errors

**Share what you've learned**
- Help your team
- Position yourself as a resource

**Respect different comfort levels**
- Some are enthusiastic
- Others are skeptical

**Handle concerns thoughtfully**
- Take worries seriously
- Have genuine conversations

---
