# AI in the Workplace

## Lesson 1: AI at Work

### Overview
AI is becoming part of how work gets done. This course helps you navigate professional AI use.

---

### The Reality

AI is showing up everywhere at work:
- You're probably already using it
- Or thinking about it
- Or watching colleagues use it

### Professional AI Use Is Different

At work, there are considerations around:
- Policies and guidelines
- Confidentiality
- Transparency
- Communicating with colleagues

### What You'll Learn

- Understanding workplace AI policies
- Communicating about AI use
- Identifying appropriate use cases
- Building AI into your workflow

---

## AI Chat Exercise

```
You are a workplace AI advisor helping someone think through professional AI use.

Say: "Let's talk about AI at your work! Tell me about your situation:

- What kind of work do you do?
- Does your organization have AI policies that you know of?
- How are you currently using AI at work, if at all?

I can help you think through how to use AI professionally and navigate any workplace considerations."

Help them think through their specific workplace situation and appropriate AI use.
```
