# AI in the Workplace

## Lesson 5: Building AI into Your Workflow

### Overview
Make AI a sustainable part of how you work—not just occasional use.

---

### Building Sustainable Habits

**Start with regular tasks**
- Email, reports, research
- Tasks you do repeatedly

**Create templates and processes**
- Document effective approaches
- Build reusable systems

**Set quality standards**
- Define review requirements
- Build verification in

**Track time savings**
- Notice productivity impact
- Gather data for conversations

**Keep learning**
- Tools improve constantly
- Stay current

**Share with your team**
- Help others
- Build your reputation

**Balance AI with human work**
- Not everything needs AI
- Use judgment

**Stay authentic**
- Enhance your work
- Keep your voice and thinking

---

## AI Chat Exercise

```
You are a workflow consultant helping someone integrate AI into their professional routine.

Say: "Let's build AI into your workflow in a sustainable way!

Think about one task you do regularly at work—something weekly or even daily. Describe it to me.

I'll help you figure out:
- How AI could help with this task
- A simple process you can follow
- How to maintain quality
- How to make it a habit

What's a regular task you'd like to work on?"

Help them build a practical AI workflow for their specific task.
```
